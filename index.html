Topic

What we propose as a team is for UNC Subs to become an application/website where consumers can pay for their streaming subscription services on a centralised platform so that it reflects as a single payment rather than multiple. You will be able to manage all your subscriptions in one place. You can access the platform on any iOS and Android device. And any web browser (excluding internet explorer).

The outcome of our initial project was to have a centralised subscription service platform. However, we concluded that more is needed to envision the goal that we have in mind. We are providing a platform that is easy for all users. The main requirement is time efficiency, so we have implanted the ability to include streaming those services on UNC Subs. The outcome is to provide a product that is effective for users and does not require individual apps to watch their favourite shows on different applications.


Motivation

Our entire motivation behind this particular "project idea" is that we do not appreciate having subscriptions withdrawn from our personal bank accounts on different monthly dates. The continuous build-up of separate, difficult-to-track charges makes budgeting significantly more complex than necessary. It is not enjoyable! We want to create a service where all your subscriptions are captured within one payment at your billing date.

Landscape

We currently have an existing competitor, which is Optus. They have a platform which is called Optus SubHub. Users can subscribe to subscription services through Optus SubHub. The differences between our product UNC Subs and Optus SubHub are that you can only subscribe to the selectively chosen services within their platform, you cannot add your own subscription platform and nor can you watch the services within their platform; you must externally access the website to be able to watch subscriptions.

The team saw Optus SubHub as a competitor, and we wanted to increase further what it is users can do within our platform verse Optus SubHub specifically being subscription based only. We've eliminated multiple applications being a prerequisite, simplifying the streaming experience for consumers.
