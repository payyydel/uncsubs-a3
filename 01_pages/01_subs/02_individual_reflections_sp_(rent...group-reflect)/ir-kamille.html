Individual Reflection – Kamille Acton

What went well?

The past few weeks of working with Omar, Josh, Clyde, Molly, and the new addition to UNC – Matthew, have been full of excitement. Since most of us have worked together in Assessment 2, communication is effortless this time. We have become more open to one another - providing feedback, asking questions, and following up with what is needed to complete our tasks.

What could be improved?

Our progress was easily tracked with the excel sheet that Omar created. Perhaps one of the things I hoped we could improve on is coming to Team meetings which we all scheduled together on the same day and at the same time. As I had some work commitments on the last meeting, I asked the Team to consider having the meeting a day before our usual meeting time and everyone agreed. However, not everyone came up and joined. Despite this, our team is still able to complete our tasks.

At least one surprising thing

The members of UNC made it easy to connect, listen to one another’s ideas, and encourage each other to achieve a common goal. We committed to fulfilling different sections of the assignment and were responsible enough to submit our contributions at the set due date.

At least one thing that you have learned about

I used to think working in groups was not doable, especially with doing everything online and never meeting in person. We used Teams as a means of communication and a central location where we upload drafts. Through this tool, we could easily track what has or has not been done and work on the same files simultaneously.
