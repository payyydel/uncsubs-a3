Group Reflection

Our group reflection has not remarkably changed from Assignment 2; however, they are key differences that have made us grow as a team together. We initially talked about how well we had worked together as a team, how friendly we were from the beginning, and willing to work together to achieve the best outcome possible. Each group member was receptive and open about our strengths and areas needing more support. People with different personalities were willing to do their part to the best of their abilities. We collaborated and allocated the work also worked well in the way that group members volunteered to contribute as much as they could.



What we have done differently from Assignment 2 for Assignments 3 and 5 is continue our weekly meetings. However, with our weekly meetings that we have continued to do, we have set specific tasks set for every single team member. The team also implemented deadlines for work required to be completed other than chasing up every member. Teams chemistry within the team had to change due to the removal of a member and a new one having to join us. Matthew is an asset to the team, considering he had to start fresh with a new project idea. He worked well with us, especially with his communication and the work he provided to the team. We are thrilled with our decision to take him along.



As stated in Assignment 2,"An area where the group could improve is time management; having different schedules and working remotely through Microsoft teams sometimes cause difficulty communicating between members about deadlines and work submissions. "The team collectively prevented this from happening again due to the new scheduling system and deadlines system we had partaken into place, we originally wanted to use Trello however it never fell through. Overall, the team has agreed that there is nothing to improve. Everyone has provided excellent work and made an effort to attend meetings. If they could not, as we all have busy schedules, we made sure for everyone to be up to date with what it is that was required to be complete, and also, others who missed out on meetings would ensure to reach out to the team members on what is required to be done.



We had to adjust to the removal of our previous team member, and with our new team member joining us, at first, we were hesitant as we were from the start when we initially started as a group. However, after our initial introductions with Matthew, we got along immediately as if he was with us for Assignment 2. Nevertheless, we were surprised by how supportive everyone was and eager to help each other and, overall, how positive the collaborative experience was especially considering we were strangers. Some team members were also surprised by how difficult of an application the Microsoft team was to use and navigate, still needing help to use Microsoft Teams after our final assessment due to the app's glitches.
