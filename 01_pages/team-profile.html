Group Processes

For Assignment 2, we collectively worked very closely with each other, creating a well-formed assessment/project. However, all groups tend to clash and have differences for various reasons, such as work commitments, lack of communication etc. And while, given the circumstances, communication left much to be desired, going into assessment 3, we've reflected on our evident shortcomings, received feedback, and strategised a method to overcome them.

Omar implemented a new process to actively show who and what is currently being worked on by section, with an element of accountability where everyone can actively view and must update their progress as they go. Pair that with our weekly check-ins and more consistent communication in teams, draft deadlines communicated group-wide in Microsoft Teams, and we've made admirable strides.
