How do the IT-specific skills in your required skill set rank in terms of demand from employers?

Using the information above, the jobs of Software Engineer, ICT Security Specialist and Security consultant share very similar IT skills as they are required to have experience in using programming languages that employers need you to know the syntax within that programming language. On the other hand, the skills necessary for an ICT Customer Support Officer mainly consist of technical support to ensure hardware and software systems are running efficiently for users. To be able to remotely support users via remote desktop software, this will ensure that users can have the utmost support from the support officers.

How do the general skills in your required skill set rank in terms of demand from employers?

Regarding the general skills, they are all very similar to each other’s position since they all fall under the IT field. All four jobs require the top 5 general skills: communication skills, problem-solving, organisational skills, writing and collaboration. Overall, it shows that the chosen jobs have essential general skills that employers highly demand.

What are the three highest-ranked IT-specific skills which are not in your required skill set?

The top three highest ranked IT-specific skills that are not within the required skill set of all jobs are, Business Analysts, Graphic Design and Business Management. This is because these skills are not relevant to any of the job positions within our group.

What are the three highest-ranked general skills which are not in your required skill set?

The three highest-ranked general skills that aren’t within the required skill set are creativity, leadership, and mentoring. The specific reason these aren’t within the required skill set of our ideal jobs within our group is considering the similarities between every job, and general skills within a professional environment don’t drastically change from job to job. In comparison to how IT-specific skills change from job to job, the requirement for a software engineer to an IT Support Officer is quite different
