Individual Reflection – Joshua Peddell

What went well?

Overall, collaboration and organisational efforts were terrific. Everyone was really receptive and ready to get involved. Everyone was really open about their interests, and perceived strengths from the word go, and tasks were volunteered for with ease and next to zero clashing.

What could be improved?

An area that left a lot to be desired was communication for specific sections. Understandably we all have different responsibilities in our day-to-day lives; however, the load of transparency and communication energies wasn’t terribly well-balanced across all members.

At least one thing that was surprising

What was most surprising about the collaborative experience was the familiarity that we all adopted with one another. Without a second of doubt, acceptance was given and equally received. The most astounding part about this is the forum. We’re all situated at different corners of the continent, and yet.

At least one thing you have learned about groups

Everyone deserves a little bit of grace. However, it’s naïve to assume that a person won’t take a mile when you give them an inch. Inevitably, the drowning man always takes someone down with them. In this instance, too much grace can indirectly affect everyone.
